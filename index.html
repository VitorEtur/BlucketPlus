How It Works

Get started with BucketPlus in just a few simple steps

1

Install Add-on

Add BucketPlus to your Google Sheets from the Google Workspace Marketplace

2

Open Sidebar

Access BucketPlus from the Extensions menu and open the color management panel

3

Select & Color

Choose your cells, pick a color, and apply instantly. Use search features for advanced coloring

4

Boost Productivity

Enjoy organized, visually appealing spreadsheets that enhance your workflow and productivity
